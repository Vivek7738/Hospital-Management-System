Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

Answering common questions and providing information about our services

What are your hospital's hours of operation?

Our hospital is open 24/7 for emergency services. Our outpatient department (OPD) operates from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. For specific department hours, please contact us directly.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept a wide range of insurance plans, including major providers such as [List a few major insurance providers]. For a comprehensive list of accepted insurance plans, please visit our insurance information page or contact our billing department.

How do I find a specialist?

You can find a specialist by visiting our website and using the "Find a Doctor" tool, where you can search by specialty, name, or condition. Alternatively, you can call our patient services team for assistance in finding the right specialist for your needs.

How do I prepare for a surgery or procedure?

Your doctor will provide specific instructions for preparing for your surgery or procedure. This may include fasting, stopping certain medications, or arranging for transportation. If you have any questions or need clarification, please contact your doctor's office directly.

How can I provide feedback about my experience at the hospital?

We value your feedback and are committed to continuously improving our services. You can provide feedback by filling out a patient satisfaction survey, which is available on our website, or by contacting our patient relations department directly.

Do you offer telemedicine services?

Yes, we offer telemedicine services for certain appointments and consultations. You can schedule a telemedicine appointment through our website or by calling our patient services team. Please ensure you have a stable internet connection and a compatible device for the virtual visit.
